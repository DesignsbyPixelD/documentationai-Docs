## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address
- Internet access
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- Optional: The Great Awakening mobile app from your app store
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and join the discipleship journey.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Go to [https://thegreatawakening.com](https://thegreatawakening.com) and click **"Join The Great Awakening"** or **"Sign Up"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and fill in your name. Agree to the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from The Great Awakening. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Log in and complete the short onboarding quiz to identify your starting point in the Discipleship Journey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Platform

Choose your preferred way to access The Great Awakening.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Open [https://thegreatawakening.com](https://thegreatawakening.com) in your browser. Log in with your credentials.
    
    Bookmark the site for quick access. Use `Ctrl`+`K` (or `Cmd`+`K` on Mac) for the global search.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
    
    ```bash
    # Search for "The Great Awakening" in your app store
    ```
    
    Log in using the same email and password.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Start the Begin Stage

Once logged in, dive into the first stage of the Discipleship Journey: **Begin**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Begin" icon="play-circle">
    From the dashboard, select **"Discipleship Journey"** and click **"Begin"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Core Lessons" icon="book-open">
    Watch the introductory video on the Gospel. Complete the reading on salvation and faith.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take the Quiz" icon="help-circle">
    Answer the questions to test your understanding. Review any missed concepts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Community" icon="users">
    Introduce yourself in the **Begin** discussion forum to connect with others.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Pro Tip: Set aside 15-20 minutes daily for your **Begin** stage to build momentum.
</Callout>

## Basic Navigation Tips

<Expandable title="Advanced Navigation Shortcuts" default-open="false">

- **Dashboard Overview**: Click the home icon to see your progress across all stages.
- **Progress Tracker**: Use the sidebar to view completion percentages for **Begin**, **Rooted**, **Belong**, **Grow**, and **Multiply**.
- **Search Scripture**: Type a Bible verse reference in the global search bar (e.g., `John 3:16`).
- **Notifications**: Enable push notifications for new lessons and community replies.

</Expandable>
