## Overview

Welcome to **The Great Awakening**, your guide to deeper faith and spiritual growth. This platform centers on the `Discipleship Journey`, a structured path that helps you grow closer to Jesus through Scripture, community, and practical steps.

You start with foundational truths and progress toward maturity and mission. Whether you are new to faith or seeking to deepen your walk, this journey meets you where you are.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Take your time with each stage. Spiritual growth happens one step at a time.
</Callout>

## Key Features and Benefits

The Great Awakening offers tools designed for steady, Scripture-centered growth:

- **Simple Path**: Clear stages prevent overwhelm.
- **Community Focus**: Connect with others at your level.
- **Scripture Deep Dives**: Study the Bible with clarity and context.

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Guided Stages | Structured progress without confusion |
| Interactive Community | Accountability and encouragement |
| Daily Scripture | Consistent growth rooted in truth |

These features target believers hungry for depth, from new converts to mature disciples.
