## Overview

Dannielle Rothering's platform guides you through **The Great Awakening**, a structured Discipleship Journey designed for steady spiritual growth. You progress through five key stages: **Begin**, **Rooted**, **Grow**, **Belong**, and **Multiply**. Each stage builds on the last, incorporating Scripture study tools, community interaction, and growth tracking to deepen your faith.

<Callout kind="info">
  Start at your current level. The platform assesses your progress and recommends the right stage to ensure steady, personalized growth.
</Callout>

## Stage Details

Dive deeper into each stage.

### Begin
<Expandable title="Awake to the Gospel" default-open="true">
  Discover Jesus' life, death, and resurrection. Key topics include the Gospel message and responding in faith.

  - What is salvation?
  - Assurance of faith
  - First steps in repentance
</Expandable>

### Rooted
<Expandable title="Rooted in Truth & Grace">
  Study core Scriptures for a unshakable foundation.

  - Grace vs. guilt
  - Identity in Christ
  - Essential Bible basics
</Expandable>

### Grow
Focus on personal maturity through daily practices.

### Belong
Join live groups and forums for fellowship.

### Multiply
Learn to mentor and share your faith effectively.
