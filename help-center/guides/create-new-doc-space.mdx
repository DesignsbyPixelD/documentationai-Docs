---
title: Create a New Documentation Space
description: Start a new project docs space in Dannielle Rothering.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Dashboard">
    Go to `https://dannielle-rothering.com` and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    On the dashboard, select "New Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Project Name">
    Give your doc space a clear, memorable name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Open your project and begin organizing your documentation!
  </Step>
</Steps>
